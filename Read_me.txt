Details of the dataset columns:

Company - laptop belongs to which company.

model_name - model name of laptop.

colour - colour of laptop.

operating_system - operating system of laptop.

processor - processor used in laptop.

processor_company - processor company.

ram_in_GB - ram size in GB.

storage_in_GB - Storage size in GB.

storage_type - storage type.

touch_screen - touchscreen available or not.

screen_size - screen size in inches.

battery_life_in_hours - battery life in hours.

delivery - product has an option of delivery or not.

price_in_Euro - price of the laptop in euros.
